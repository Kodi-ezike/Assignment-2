Pancakes

I love this because it is easy to make.

List of ingredients

  • sugar
  • milk
  • flour
  • egg
  • groundnut oil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and sugar
  2. Add milk and eggs
  3. Add water and mix to a fine mixture
  4. Pour a teaspoon of oil into a pan and fry the pancakes

Fried plantain

List of ingredients

  • plantain
  • salt
  • pepper
  • groundnut oil

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the plantain
  2. Cut into small pieces
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper to your taste
  4. Pour oil into the frying pan and fry the plantain

Toast bread

This is one my best choices for breakfast

List of ingredients

  • bread
  • eggs
  • salt
  • butter
  • maggi

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl
  2. Add salt and maggi to the eggs and mix properly
  3. Apply butter to one side of the sliced break and place the greasy part on the toaster
  4. Spread two spoons of the egg mixture on the bread
  5. Toast the bread using the toaster

Yam porridge

This is really delicious and quick to prepare

List of ingredients

  • yam
  • salt
  • maggi
  • red oil
  • smoked fish
  • pepper
  • crayfish

Steps

  1. Peel the yam and cut into small pieces
  2. Put the yam into a bowl of water
  3. Wash the yam and add the right amount of water and put it it on fire
  4. Wash the smoked fish and add it to the yam>
  5. Add ground pepper, crayfish, salt, maggi and red oil to the yam
  6. Turn and leave it to cook for a few minutes
